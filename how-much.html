How much an agency can receive

The monthly amount for the Children's special allowances (CSA) is equal to the maximum amount for the Canada child benefit (CCB), based on the child's age (under 6, and 6 through 17). The CSA may also include a supplement amount for the Child disability benefit (CDB), if the child is eligible for the Disability tax credit (DTC).
