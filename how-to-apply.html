How to apply

Follow these steps to apply for the Children's special allowances (CSA)

On this page

  • Register your agency
    • Register by mail, fax, or in-person
  • Start or end a CSA payment
    • Send forms by mail
    • Send forms electronically

Register your agency

To receive payments for the CSA your agency must have a business number assigned specifically for CSA purposes.

What the CSA business number will look like

The CSA-BN number will have the following format 123456789 RA0001.

  • The first nine digits identify the recipient which is unique to a business or legal entity
  • The last six alphanumeric characters identify the CSA program

Register by mail, or fax

  1. Write a letter indicating your desire to register for the CSA, include your agency name and a method of contact
    1. If you have a business number, you can include this in the letter, otherwise one will be created as part of the process
  2. Mail, fax or drop it off to your nearest tax services office or tax centre
    1. To find the appropriate office or centre, go to Find a CRA address.
    2. Avoid delays by including copies of your agency’s supporting documentation

    3. Supporting documentation

    If you are a non-governmental agency you will need to provide:

    • a copy of your provincial or territorial licence or authorization regarding the protection, custody and care of children.

    If you are authorized by an Indigenous governing body you will need to provide:

    • proof of legislative authority regarding the protection, custody and care of children
    Accepted documents for agencies authorized by an Indigenous governing body

    • providing a notice of intent to exercise legislative authority; or
    • requesting to enter into a coordination agreement; and
    • having Indigenous Laws relating to child and family services

Start or end a CSA payment

Once you have registered a CSA business number, you can apply for the CSA. An agency is required to apply for:

  • each child for whom they want to receive the CSA
  • each child for whom the CSA should be cancelled

To avoid delays in processing your application, please take care to provide complete information.

In the event of a delay, retroactive payments are possible but note that there is a limit on retroactive payments.

Send forms by mail

  1. Download and complete the Form RC64, Children's special allowances,
  2. Forms can be mailed to the nearest tax centre that serves your area.

Send forms electronically

To register for electronic filing options, please call our toll-free CSA on the net helpdesk at 1-877-418-7714

  • We will email you the CSA on the net enrollment package, which includes:
    • the registration form
    • a copy of the CSA electronic filing document, which contains more details and instructions

Send us the completed registration forms and supporting documentation by the electronic application method we set you up with on the phone (such as secure web site or FTP).
